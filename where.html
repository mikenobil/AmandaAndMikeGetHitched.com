Planes, Trains and Automobiles
(but mostly planes)

Most of our guests will be flying in from various parts of the country, so here are some recommendations for you:

  • Reno-Tahoe Airport
    • Reno-Tahoe airport is just over an hour from South Lake Tahoe
      Directions
    • Shuttle busses go from the Reno-Tahoe airport to South Lake Tahoe Casinos where the Lakeland Village courtesy shuttle will pick you up free of charge.
      South Tahoe Express
    • Rental cars may be a good option, too. We've always had good luck using Kayak.com to rent cars.
  • Sacramento Airport
    • Sacramento airport is about 2 hours from South Lake Tahoe.
      Directions
    • We haven't seen or heard of shuttle busses that run from Sacramento to Tahoe, so rental cars will be your best bet if you fly into Sacramento.
  • San Francisco Airport, Oakland Airport and San Jose Airport
    • The San Francisco, Oakland and San Jose Airports are all between 3 and 4 hours from South Lake Tahoe.
    • We haven't seen or heard of shuttle busses that run from these airports to Tahoe, so rental cars will be your best bet if you fly into these airports.

Things To Do

Lake Tahoe is one of our favorite places on earth, mostly because there is so much to do, both indoors and outdoors.

  • Emerald Bay is one of those places that can't be explained with words, you'll just have to see it yourself.
  • Skiing and Snowboarding Lake Tahoe provides some of the best snow and terrain in the country. Even during the summer months, the ski resorts have tons of activities for all ages.
  • If you're the gambling type these are the 3 casinos we recommend: Harrah's, Harvey's and MontBleu.
  • And you'll find plenty of restaurants, bars, spas, shops and activities in Stateline, CA and Stateline, NV, most concentrated around the Heavenly Gondola.
